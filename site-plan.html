RiffMaster Site Plan

Javier Guerrero

Site Name

RiffMaster

I chose this name because it combines the core of rock music (the "Riff") with the goal of every musician: achieving mastery. It is short, powerful, and easy to remember for the guitar community.

Site Purpose

RiffMaster aims to centralize resources for guitarists, offering technical lessons, equipment reviews (pedals, amplifiers), and a community space where musicians can share knowledge and improve their skills.

Scenarios

  • Gear Advice: "I am a beginner guitarist looking for a local or online guide to help me choose my first electric guitar and amplifier without overspending. Does RiffMaster have a 'Beginner's Gear Guide'?"
  • Chord Mastery: "I want to learn how to play rhythm guitar and I am struggling with barre chords. Does this site provide a library of basic and advanced chord shapes along with tips on how to transition between them smoothly?"

Color Scheme

The color palette is inspired by classic amplifier brands, using high contrast for a professional "Dark Mode" look.

Primary: #f73642 (Rock Red)
Background: #121212 (Deep Black)
Text/Accent: #F1FAEE (Cream White)

Typography

Bebas Neue: Used for Headings (H1, H2, H3).

Lexend: Used for body text, paragraphs, and navigation for its excellent readability.

Wireframe

The layout focuses on a clean design that highlights video lessons and gear imagery.
